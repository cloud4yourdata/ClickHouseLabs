# Timestamp
---
## Types
---
 - Date [1970-01-01, 2149-06-06]
 - Date32 [1900-01-01 00:00:00, 2299-12-31 23:59:59]
 - Time [-999:59:59, 999:59:59] Resolution: 1 second.
 - DateTime [1970-01-01 00:00:00, 2106-02-07 06:28:15] Resolution: 1 second.
 - Time64  [-999:59:59.000, 999:59:59.999] for precision = 3
 - DateTime64  [1900-01-01 00:00:00, 2299-12-31 23:59:59.999999999]
---
## Timestamp functions
---
- UTCTimestamp() now('UTC')
- YYYYMMDDToDate(YYYYMMDD)
- YYYYMMDDToDate32(YYYYMMDD)
